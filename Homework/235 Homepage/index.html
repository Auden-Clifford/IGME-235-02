About Me

I'm Auden Clifford. I've wanted to make video games since I was very young and my love for games in general goes way back. Some of my favorite games include Minecraft, Cyberpunk 2077, and Half-Life: Alyx. Aside from video games I'm a huge fan of TTRPGs and am currently getting into Call of Cthulhu. I also occasionally enjoy sewing, playing the fiddle, and 18th-century reenacting.

My hope is that my time at RIT will give me the knowledge and experience necessary to excel in the professional world of game design. For this class more specifically I hope to be able to take away some portfolio pieces that might help me get a job in the future.

Once I graduate, I plan to work as a professional in the industry long enough to pay off my debts and achieve some financial security before eventually breaking off to start my own company.
